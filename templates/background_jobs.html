Periodic Scraping

Automatically collects questions from educational websites and coding platforms. Runs every hour by default to keep the database updated with fresh content.

Periodic Classification

Uses BERT and other ML models to automatically classify question difficulty and categorize topics. Processes unclassified questions every 30 minutes.

Question Generation

Uses T5 transformer models to generate new questions from predefined topics. Runs every 6 hours to continuously expand the question database.

Cloud Sync

Synchronizes questions with cloud database (Supabase) for backup and multi-instance deployment. Runs every 12 hours when cloud credentials are configured.
